FAQ'S

1. How does Engage reach out to the candidates ?

Engage integrates into your ATS and automatically makes contact with the candidates over WhatsApp. Incase of no existing ATS, you can upload the candidates information spreadsheet here.

2. Can I customise my greeting messages ?

Absolutely, your greeting messages and shortlisting notifications can be customised to represent your company's identity. Login to the dashboard and under Engage tab click on customise.

3. How can I upload my candidate file incase of no ATS?

You can upload a CSV, Excel or a spreadsheet file on a continuous basis. Write to us at tech@gethyre.in to discuss ATS integrations.

4. Can Engage evaluate for a candidate's fit ?

Before Engage is deployed, we have a brief questionnaire for you to answer. Based on that Engage sends out relevant snippets to gather information and evaluate a best fit score.

5. Does Engage evaluate the candidate on his psychometric abilities ?

Yes, psychometric evaluation is an integral part of Engage. We have a well researched questionnaire which calls for a comprehensive psychometric evaluation of the candidate. If you would like to include your own questions or are looking for selective personlaity traits, please write to us at partner@gethyre.in

6. How are interviews scheduled ?

In your get:Hyre web dashboard, under the engage tab you are asked for your time slots. Engage uses this to negotiate an appropriate time with the candidate and schedule further processes. It then sends out reminder notifications prior to the interview

5. Am I charged on the number of persons hired ?

No. you wouldn't be charged anything on a candidate's employment confirmation. For more information pricing click here

8. Can I upgrade my plan?

Yes, you can. We encourage you to try our advanced version as it includes a comprehensive solution set. Go to the pricing tab in your dashboard after logging in.

9. Can I cancel or put my current plan on hold ?

You definitely can put your current plan on hold. You can view ongoing campaigns in the overview tab of your dashboard. You can also cancel your plan whenever, but you do have a tough mandatory exit interview to go through.

10. What happens to the uploaded candidate information ?

Your information is confidential and is stored in our secure databases online. At no point are we authorised to sell or duplicate your information.

11. Does Engage verify the candidates information ?

Engage has a built in data validation tool which saves you a lot of time. You can view the status of all the candidates under the visualisation tab in your dashboard.

12. How can I shortlist candidates ?

The comprehensive prehire suite of engage includes fit score, availability, pyschometric evaluation and data validation. All this is present under the visualisation tab which shows you a graph view and a candidate view.

13. Can I shortlist candidates automatically ?

Yes, you can pre-define your benchmarks on various criteria to shortlist automatically.
