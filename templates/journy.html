FAQ'S

1.Which roles can I hire for with Journy ?

Journy is the perfect solution for all your non-tech roles. Customization is also feasible for your role, domain and company in particular.

2. How does Journy help me hire ?

Journy's is a conversational AI and is trained on domain specific data. It conducts multiple conversations with candidates based on the selected theme and hiring role. It them evaluayes these conversations to arrive at various business critical performance metrics. You can view the evaluation criteria here and the theme list here

3. Can I customise Journy for my particular domain and role ?

There's is an inbuilt domain trained Journy for your non tech role. You can build on the existing Journy to customise for your particular domain and company. Customisation is subject to your pricing plan and is visible under the Journy's tab of your dashboard

4. Can I also use Journy's to train my employees ?

Yes. Journy's is an excellent tool for training your employees on your domain and the job. Please write to us at journy@gethyre.in with your exact requirements

5. How do I activate Journy's ?

On the confirmation of your payment, you will receive your login link and password for your dashboard. You can add Journy's to your hiring blueprint in the dashboard.

6. Where is Journy's conducted?

Journy's sends out web links to candidates on WhatsApp. The duration of each Journy varies between 15 min to 40 min based on your role and the progress is visible in your web dashboard.

7. How does Journy's ensure an intelligent and responsive conversation ?

Journy's is built on state of the art NLP and NLG algorithms and is conversational in nature. Journy's is trained on domain specific data and is also responsive in accordance with the conversation. For instance in Journy which involves a conversation between a salesperson and a client, the client's response is dependent on the salesperson's conversation, negotiation and overall sentiment among other criteria. To view the full list of criteria click here.

8. Can I only purchase Journy's?

Yes, you can. But we do encourage you to team it up with Engage for a comprehensive evaluation.

9. Can I set my benchmarks to shortlist with Journy?

Yes, you can pre-set your benchmarks according to your relevant criteria. Benchmarks are set on your dashboard and you can view the evaluation criteria here.
